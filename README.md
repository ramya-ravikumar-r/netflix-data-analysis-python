# 📊 Netflix Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Netflix dataset to uncover patterns in content distribution, genres, ratings, and growth trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
* Identify top contributing countries
* Understand content growth over time
* Explore most popular genres
* Analyze content ratings and duration

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Movies dominate Netflix content (~69%) compared to TV Shows (~31%)
* United States contributes the highest number of titles
* Content growth increased significantly after 2015
* International Movies, Dramas, and Comedies are the most popular genres
* Most movies are between 80–120 minutes long
* TV-MA is the most common rating, indicating mature audience focus

---

## 📈 Conclusion

Netflix's content strategy focuses on globally diverse, mature, and movie-dominated content. The platform has shown rapid growth in content production after 2015, with strong emphasis on international markets and audience preferences.

---
